Privacy Policy

How Over Exposure Productions collects, uses and stores your data — in plain language.

Last updated: 2026

1. Who we are

Over Exposure Productions (“we”, “us”) is a production and film-servicing company based at Yas Creative Hub, Yas Island — Abu Dhabi. This policy explains what data we collect through this website and how we handle it. Questions? Email info@ox.productionsuae.com.

2. What we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit the contact form we receive your name, email, phone (if provided), project type and message.
  • Cookies & local storage. We use essential browser storage to remember your cookie choice and to keep this preview session unlocked. We may use limited, aggregate analytics to understand how the site is used.
  • Technical data. Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) are recorded by our host for security and performance.

3. How we use it

We use your information solely to respond to enquiries, provide and improve our services, and keep the site secure. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. How we store it

Contact enquiries are delivered to info@ox.productionsuae.com and a backup copy is stored on our host in a location that is never served publicly. Data is retained only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and meet legal obligations, then deleted. Reasonable technical and organisational measures protect it against loss or unauthorised access.

5. Cookies & your choices

When you click Accept on our consent banner, we store that choice in your browser so we don’t ask again. Essential storage needed to run the site cannot be switched off, but you can clear cookies and local storage at any time from your browser settings. Declining non-essential analytics will not affect your ability to use the site.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you. To do so, contact us at info@ox.productionsuae.com and we will respond within a reasonable period.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “last updated” date above.
